8 : 1 Multiplexer


In the 8 to 1 multiplexer, there are total

eight inputs, i.e., A0, A1, A2, A3, A4, A5, A6, and A7,

3 selection lines, i.e., S0, S1and S2

and single output, i.e., Y.

On the basis of the combination of inputs that are present at the selection lines S0, S1, and S2, one of these 8 inputs are connected to the output.

the Boolean equation for the output is given as:

Y = ~S0 ~S1 ~S2 D0 + ~S0 ~S1 S2 D1 + ~S0 S1 ~S2 D2 + ~S0 S1 S2 D3 + S0 ~S1 ~S2 D4 + S0 ~S1 S2 D5 + S0 S1 ~S2 D6 + S0 S1 S2 D7
