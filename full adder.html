Full Adder And Subtractor


FULL ADDER

The first two inputs are A and B and the third input is an input carry as C-IN.

The output carry is designated as C-OUT and the normal output is designated as S which is SUM.

SUM = C-IN XOR (A XOR B)

COUT = AB + C-IN(A XOR B)


FULL SUBTRACTOR

The three inputs A, B and Bin, denote the minuend, subtrahend, and previous borrow, respectively.

The two outputs, D and Bout represent the difference and output borrow, respectively.

D = (A XOR B) XOR Bin

Bout= A’Bin + A’B + BBin
